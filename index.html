WHO I AM

Innovate

I'm an experienced software engineer driven by a desire to push the boundaries of what is possible.

Holding a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Colorado, Denver, I aim to build innovative solutions that seamlessly bridge the gap between creativity and functionality and drive progress in the digital landscape.

Inspire

As a former musician who holds a BA in Music Performance, a teacher, and founder of a music school, I've had the privilege of inspiring students of all ages to reach their full potential in music. Through leadership and a team-oriented approach, I fostered an environment where creativity flourished and students were empowered to become better musicians.

This experience has influenced my perspective as a software engineer, instilling in me a commitment to inspire and lead by example, fostering collaboration and excellence in every project.

Impact

As an avid trail runner, I've tackled challenging ultra-marathons, including the Leadville 100-mile trail run, the Bighorn 50-mile trail run, and The Grande Traverse from Crested Butte to Aspen.

These experiences have taught me valuable lessons that I apply to my work as a software engineer. Just as ultra-marathons are not completed in one day but are a culmination of small efforts over the course of training and building up to race day, software engineering projects require patience, persistence, and a dedication to the journey. My trail running endeavors have instilled in me a mindset of resilience, adaptability, and continuous improvement, qualities that I bring to every project to make a lasting impact.
